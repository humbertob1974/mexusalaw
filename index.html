What We Do

Legal Services Across
Both Borders

Founded by Veronica de la Fuente, MexUSA Law Firm specializes in family-based U.S. immigration and Mexican Law — helping clients around the world immigrate, reunite with families, and resolve legal matters in both nations.

United States

U.S. Immigration Law

We help clients around the world legally immigrate to the United States and reunite with their families.

  • Family petitions & consular process
  • Waivers of inadmissibility
  • Adjustment of Status
  • Citizenship & certificate of citizenship
  • Green Card renewal / removal of conditions
  • Deportation defense & DACA
  • Employment Authorization & Travel Document
  • FOIA & U.S. Visas
México

Mexican Law

We provide legal counsel to Mexican citizens living in the U.S. and validate and enforce Mexican orders in American courts.

  • Validation of foreign decrees in US & Mexico
  • Recording of foreign orders
  • Enforcement of Mexican court orders in the U.S.
  • General counseling in Mexican Law
  • Family cases linked to immigration matters

Meet the Attorney

Veronica de la Fuente, Esq.

U.S. & Mexico Licensed Attorney · San Antonio, Texas

The daughter of immigrants who came to the United States seeking a better future, Veronica grew up in Mexico and returned to the U.S. in 2009 as a citizen — facing firsthand the same challenges every immigrant encounters. That lived experience fuels her dedication to immigration and international law. After working as a paralegal in immigration, she pursued dual licensure to give clients full legal representation on both sides of the border.
